# Track Description: Blockchain for Good Alliance (BGA)

## Overview

This track focuses on the intersection of Web3 technology and real-world impact. Backed by the **Blockchain for Good Alliance (BGA)**—the flagship initiative founded by Bybit (the world's second-largest crypto exchange)—this track challenges builders to accelerate the adoption of blockchain solutions that address global social, economic, and public infrastructure challenges.

Projects in this track will explore how decentralized technology and distributed AI can be scaled in collaboration with governments, international organizations, and enterprise partners to drive meaningful, real-world change.

## Key Focus Areas for Builders

To align with the judging panel's expertise, participants should consider focusing on:

- **Public Infrastructure & Social Impact** — Solutions designed to scale with government or NGO/UN collaboration.
- **Real-World Blockchain Adoption** — Practical applications that solve tangible, everyday economic or social problems.
- **Decentralized AI Integration** — Incorporating distributed AI or federated learning frameworks to solve complex data and privacy challenges.
